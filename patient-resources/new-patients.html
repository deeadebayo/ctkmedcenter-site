Your first visit

Preparing for your doctor's appointment



Let's make your visit to the doctor as pleasant as possible

At CTKMC, our main goal is to make your visit and treatment as relaxed as possible while providing you with high quality, compassionate medical care. We understand that visiting the doctor can be stressful, but planning ahead can help relieve some of that anxiety.


Pre-Visit Checklist

There's just a few things you can do prior to your appointment to help us keep up on your health and for you to get the most out of your visit. Please consider this short "to do" list:

Make sure to bring these key items:

  • a valid photo id

  • your insurance card

  • copies of records from doctors related to your visit with us


Make a list of your health questions & concerns:

If you write down a list of your health questions or concerns, it will help both you and the doctor plan your visit. In addition, it will minimize the chance of forgetting important medical concerns and ensuring there is adequate time to fully address them.


Bring a friend or a family member:

If you are worried, bring a second set of ears. Your concerned friend or relative will help you remember what was discussed.


Bring a list of your current medications:

You can also just bring in your medication bottles. At almost every visit, the doctor will talk with you about your medications. Making sure everyone has a common understand about what you are taking is essential to providing you with high quality care.


Thank you for considering these ideas prior to your doctor's appointment. To put you at ease before you come to the office, we suggest you read over our What to Expect page, where we explain the experience you should expect from us. We look forward to seeing you soon.
