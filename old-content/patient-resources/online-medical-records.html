Online medical records

Keeping track of your medical records



Simple, online access to your health records



We have partnered with Practice Fusion to give you access to all your health records currently on file at our location.

Remember when banks started offering online banking? Before, we used to call the bank for everything. But now, millions of people choose to manage their bank account online.

Your online patient portal is like an online bank account for all your health information.

   

Convenient Access

One login to manage all your health data in one place from any device.

Simple Interface

The web interface makes it easy to find what you’re looking for.

Control Your Information

Need to update your address or phone number? Patient Fusion makes it easy.

Private and Secure

Our system is safe – your information is covered by the same HIPAA safeguards we use at our office.

Test Results

Review all your lab and diagnostic text results at your convenience

Vital Signs

Keep track of your vital signs (from each visit) over time ‑ including weight and blood pressure.

Current Information

Find a list of your medications, present immunizations, allergies, and care plans.
