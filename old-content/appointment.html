What time works for you?

In the schedule below, choose your desired appointment time (e.g. '10:30 a.m.') and click the arrow to the right. On the next screens, you'll be asked to enter your name, date of birth, and contact information.

What's next?

When your appointment is confirmed, you will receive an email with the appointment details. If for any chance our office has to reschedule your appointment, you will receive an email with some proposed times. Alternatively, if you need a different time you can contact our office directly.

A day before your appointment, our office will call you to confirm your visit on the next day.




*For same-day appointment, it may be quicker to call the office at 770-554-8015, instead.
*If for any reason, you are having difficulty using this scheduler, feel free to reach us at 770-554-8015 to schedule your appointment.
*As a courtesy, if for any reason you need to cancel or reschedule an appointment, call our office.
